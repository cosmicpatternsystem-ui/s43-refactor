# 1. Executive Charter

S43 is defined as a strategic intelligence and allocation platform designed to evolve from a working analytical engine into a durable, institution-grade macro decision system.

- ingesting and organizing macro-financial signals,

- preserving continuity across versions,

## 5.3 Refactor / Structural Evolution Source

This role is reserved for deeper architectural refactoring and structural evolution.

Typical root:

text

This source line may contain architectural improvements, but it must not be blindly merged into the main operational source.

## 5.4 Non-Negotiable Rule

No direct merge is allowed from the refactor source into the operational source.

Every transition must pass:

- compile validation,
- functional validation,
- diff review,
- backup creation,
- rollback plan,
- and continuity check against known-good references.
