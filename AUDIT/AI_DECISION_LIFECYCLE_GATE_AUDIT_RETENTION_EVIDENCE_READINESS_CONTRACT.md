# AI Decision Lifecycle Gate Audit Retention Evidence Readiness Contract

## Purpose
Define the minimum evidence readiness expectations required before lifecycle gate audit retention governance can be considered promotable toward future enforcement.

## Evidence Requirements
Evidence readiness should include, at minimum:
- a reference to the applicable retention governance source,
- a reference to the relevant lifecycle gate audit reporting source,
- a declared retention expectation,
- a declared immutability expectation,
- a declared review window,
- a traceable owner or governance authority,
- any associated exception record when readiness is incomplete.

## Readiness Interpretation
Evidence is considered readiness-sufficient when a reviewer can determine:
- what must be retained,
- how long or under what review expectation it remains governed,
- whether immutability is expected,
- whether the record is ready, conditionally ready, or not ready,
- what missing evidence or exception path prevents promotion if readiness is incomplete.

## Evidence Gaps
Evidence should be considered insufficient when:
- governance claims cannot be traced to a source document,
- a readiness label exists without supporting rationale,
- review windows are mentioned without ownership,
- immutability is implied but not declared,
- exceptions are referenced but not linked.

## Documentation-Only Constraint
This contract establishes evidence readiness expectations only. It does not implement policy gates, automated evidence collection, or runtime enforcement.
